The Best Movies According To Yahya Masood

My top 3 movies of all-time are the following:

Bullet Train

This is my number one movie, mostly because of all the details they put into every character and their backstory. Its a very funny movie as well and always brings a smile to my face.

Red Notice

Has got two of my favourite actors, Dwayne Johnson and Ryan Reynolds and them working together is absolutely hilarious.

Fast 5

Love this movie mainly because im a fan of the whole series and cars in general.
